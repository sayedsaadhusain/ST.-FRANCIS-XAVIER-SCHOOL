🏫 Our Story

About St. Francis Xavier School

Founded in 2002, St. Francis Xavier School stands as a beacon of academic excellence in Lucknow. Located on Cantt Road, Lalbagh, our institution has been nurturing young minds through a perfect blend of the rigorous ICSE curriculum and holistic character development.

We believe education is not just about academic achievement — it's about shaping responsible, compassionate, and future-ready individuals who contribute meaningfully to society.

🎯 Our Mission
Provide world-class education that cultivates intellectual curiosity, moral integrity, and a lifelong love of learning.
🔭 Our Vision
To be the premier institution that prepares students to lead, innovate, and serve in a global society.
💎 Core Values
Excellence, Integrity, Compassion, Discipline, Innovation — principles that guide every aspect of school life.
🌟 Our Approach
Student-centered learning that balances academics with co-curricular activities for all-round development.

📘 Curriculum

Academic Programs

Nurturing curiosity, logical reasoning, and a passion for learning from nursery through senior board examinations.

Nursery to Class V

Foundational & Primary Education

Our primary school curriculum centers on building strong foundational blocks in language, logical mathematics, and environmental understanding. Through structured student-centric activities, we inculcate moral values and basic life skills.

We promote play-based inquiry for pre-primary students, moving progressively to comprehensive analytical learning models in higher primary levels.

Key Outcomes & Core Subjects

  • Subjects: English, Mathematics, Environmental Studies (EVS), Hindi, Computer Science, General Knowledge.
  • Language Proficiency: Advanced reading comprehension, phonetics, and clear verbal communication.
  • Logical Reasoning: Fundamental mathematical problem solving and operational arithmetic.
  • Co-Curriculars: Physical education, vocal music, sketching, storytelling, and soft-skills workshop sessions.
Class VI to VIII

Preparatory Middle Years

Middle school is a transition phase designed to guide students from observational learning to critical, concept-based analysis. We introduce departmentalized instruction where expert educators focus on independent subject disciplines.

We lay absolute priority on scientific experiments, digital computing fundamentals, and literature analysis, aligning students towards board preparations.

Key Outcomes & Core Subjects

  • Subjects: English Language & Literature, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History & Civics, Geography, Hindi, computer applications.
  • Experimental Science: Practical demonstration sessions in dedicated laboratory spaces.
  • Critical Reading: Comprehensive literary text studies and advanced creative composition.
  • Technical Skills: Introduction to algorithms, basic programming syntax, and office automation software.
Class IX & X

Rigorous ICSE Board Program

Designed in strict compliance with the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE). The ICSE syllabus challenges students to apply comprehensive knowledge and develop deep problem-solving insights across all subjects.

Extensive preparatory sessions, model mock exams, and personalized remedial tracking support students in achieving stellar board standings.

Key Outcomes & Board Focus

  • Stream Tracks: Focused tracks in Science and Commerce subjects with multi-disciplinary electives.
  • Analytical Application: Direct practice of past board question structures and systematic scoring guidance.
  • Computer Coding: In-depth object-oriented programming module using Java.
  • Career Guidance: Stream selection assessments, higher-education prep counsel, and character leadership certificates.

📝 Enrolment

Admission Process

Joining St. Francis Xavier School is the first step toward a bright academic journey. Our admission process is simple, structured, and transparent.

01

Inquiry Form submission

Fill out the basic admission inquiry form (provided to the right) with student details, contact numbers, and seeking grade.

02

Document Submission

Our admissions desk registers your application. You will be requested to submit student birth certificate, transfer certificates, and previous progress logs.

03

Assessment & Interaction

An interactive, stress-free assessment is scheduled for the student, followed by a friendly chat with parents to align values.

04

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Upon verification of assessment results and successful document reviews, the fee structure is paid to confirm registration.
